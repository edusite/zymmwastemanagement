How to recycle the things

Why recycle?

  • Reduces waste

    Recycling cuts down the amount of rubbish sent to landfills, helping keep our surroundings clean.

  • Saves natural resources

    It reduces the need to use raw materials like trees, water, and minerals.

  • Saves energy

    Producing items from recycled materials uses less energy than making them from scratch.

  • Protects the environment

    Recycling lowers pollution and helps reduce the impact of climate change.

Steps for recycling

Collecting

Recyclable materials are gathered from homes, schools, and public bins.This includes items like plastic bottles, paper, glass, and metal cans.

Sorting

The collected waste is separated into different types.For example, plastic is separated from paper and glass so each material can be processed correctly.

Processing

The materials are cleaned and broken down.They are turned into raw materials (like melted plastic or pulp from paper) so they can be reused.

Manufacturing

The recycled materials are used to make new products.For example, plastic bottles can become new containers, and paper can become notebooks.

Things you can recycle

Plastic

Bottles, containers, packaging , etc..

Paper

Newspapers, notebooks, cardboard , etc..

Glass

Bottles and jars , etc..

Metal

Cans (like soda or food cans)

Tips for better recycling

  • Sort and rinse recyclables.
  • Use reusables, avoid single-use.
  • Encourage others to recycle.
